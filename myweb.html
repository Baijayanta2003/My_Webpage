Hi, I am Baijayanta Bhattacharyya

Astrophysicist | Researcher | Explorer of the Cosmos

Junior Research Fellow at IIT Kharagpur

I work in radio cosmology. My focus is on the 21 cm signal, which comes from neutral hydrogen in the Universe. By studying it, we can learn how the first stars and galaxies formed and how the Universe changed over time. Most of my work is about handling and refining the huge amounts of data collected by radio telescopes, trying to pull out this faint signal from all the noise around it.
